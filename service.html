Our Services

At Cindo Barbershop, we pride ourselves on offering personalized services tailored to meet your unique grooming needs. Our goal is to provide you with an exceptional experience and results that exceed your expectations. Visit us today and discover the Cindo Barbershop difference.

Haircut

Our skilled barbers are adept at providing a wide variety of haircuts, from classic styles to modern trends. Whether you're looking for a sharp fade, a clean undercut, or a traditional scissor cut, we take the time to understand your preferences and deliver a cut that suits your individual style and face shape. Each haircut includes a consultation to ensure we achieve the look you desire.

Hairwash

Experience the ultimate relaxation with our hairwash service. Using premium shampoos and conditioners, we thoroughly cleanse your hair and scalp, removing dirt, oil, and product buildup. Our gentle scalp massage during the wash stimulates blood flow and promotes relaxation, leaving your hair feeling fresh, clean, and revitalized.

Hair color

Whether you're looking to cover grays, enhance your natural shade, or try a bold new color, our hair color services have you covered. We use high-quality, professional-grade products to ensure vibrant, long-lasting results. Our color specialists work with you to select the perfect shade and technique, whether it's a full color, highlights, lowlights, or balayage. Each color service includes a consultation to discuss your desired look and maintain the health and integrity of your hair.

Beard Trims

Shaping, grooming, and maintenance of facial hair.

Shaves

Straight razor shaves, often with hot towels and soothing balms.

Personalized Service

Every consultation is personalized based on the customers needs and preferences.
